# Shaleio Guild Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** Upon Signature
**Parties:** Shaleio LLC ("Guild") and the undersigned individual or entity ("Recipient")

---

## 1. Purpose

The Recipient seeks entry into the Shaleio Guild and, as part of the Path of the Builder, may receive access to confidential information, intellectual property, internal repositories, operational processes, business models, financial details, product specifications, and other sensitive materials belonging to Shaleio LLC or individual Builders.

This NDA governs the protection, handling, and permitted uses of such information.

---

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" includes, but is not limited to:

* Internal repositories, utilities, modules, Strata, reforges, or lineage records.
* Documentation, architectural patterns, deployment workflows, IaC materials, or technical specifications.
* Business plans, guild structures, pricing strategies, revenue models, SGL mechanics, or legal frameworks.
* Discussions held in private Guild channels including Discord, calls, or written communication.
* Any information designated as confidential orally or in writing.

Confidential Information **does not** include information that:

* Is or becomes public through no fault of the Recipient;
* Is independently developed without Guild materials;
* Is lawfully obtained from a third party without breach of confidentiality;
* Must be disclosed by law (with prior written notice to Shaleio LLC when legally permitted).

---

## 3. Obligations of Recipient

The Recipient agrees to:

* Maintain the confidentiality of all Confidential Information with the same care used to protect their own confidential materials, but no less than reasonable care.
* Use Confidential Information **solely** for participation in the Shaleio Guild.
* Not disclose, share, distribute, publish, or make available Confidential Information to any third party without written permission from Shaleio LLC.
* Not reproduce, clone, fork, mirror, or derive competing works from Confidential Information outside the permissions granted by the Guild or applicable SGL.
* Immediately notify Shaleio LLC of any unauthorized disclosure or suspected breach.

---

## 4. Intellectual Property Ownership

All Confidential Information remains the sole property of Shaleio LLC or the originating Builder.

This NDA does **not** grant:

* ownership rights,
* licenses, or
* commercial usage rights,

except where explicitly granted through separate documents such as the Shaleio Guild License (SGL) or individual agreements.

---

## 5. Duration

All confidentiality obligations remain in force for **five (5) years** from the date of signature or **two (2) years after Recipient exits the Guild**, whichever is later.

Certain proprietary frameworks, Strata patterns, and core utilities designated as "Perpetually Confidential" remain protected indefinitely.

---

## 6. Return or Destruction of Materials

Upon request or upon leaving the Guild, the Recipient must:

* return all Confidential Information, or
* certify its secure destruction.

No local copies, forks, screenshots, or derivative artifacts may be retained unless explicitly permitted by the Guild.

---

## 7. Remedies

A breach of this NDA may cause irreparable harm to Shaleio LLC and individual Builders.

The Guild may seek:

* injunctive relief,
* legal damages,
* termination of Guild access,
* forfeiture of bounties or pending royalties,
* removal from lineage or recognition,
* and other remedies permitted by law.

---

## 8. Non-Circumvention

The Recipient agrees not to bypass the Guild by:

* soliciting Guild clients,
* replicating Guild products for external sale,
* undermining Guild governance,
* or entering into competitive agreements derived from Confidential Information.

---

## 9. Independent Builders

The Recipient acknowledges that:

* Builders are independent entities,
* the Guild is not an employer (unless separately contracted),
* each Builder retains their own IP,
* and the Guild's confidentiality extends to protecting **Builder-owned** IP as well.
