# Shaleio Guild Codex: Ranks & Pathways

## Unproven

Those outside the Guild. Unproven individuals have no access to internal systems, resources, or discussions beyond the public Town Square. They may observe the Guild from a distance but hold no status, permissions, or recognition.

## Initiate (LLC Entity Associated with ShaleIO LLC)

An individual who has formally begun the Path of the Builder.

**Requirements:**

* Operates through an independent LLC.
* Has signed the Charter, NDA, and all required entry documents.
* Has declared intent to craft within the Guild ecosystem.

**Access & Limitations:**

* Base access to the Guild Discord.
* No access to internal repositories, utilities, or Guild infrastructure.
* Must present credible talent (e.g., GitHub portfolio) **or** provide meaningful assistance to an existing Builder in a B2B capacity.
* May observe, learn, and prepare for advancement, but does not yet contribute to Strata or internal Guildwork.

## Forger

A rising craftsman who has demonstrated baseline capability and reliability.

**Requirements:**

* Proven talent through submitted artifacts, utilities, or bounties.
* Endorsed by at least one Builder or Steward.

**Access & Privileges:**

* Read access to internal repositories under least-privilege principles.
* May submit improvements to Guild general utilities.
* Eligible for bounty work.
* May participate in supervised reforges.

**Limitations:**

* Holds no royalty slices.
* Cannot lead reforges or craft Strata independently.

## Builder

A full Builder of the Guild, entrusted with craft, contribution, and the shaping of Strata.

**Requirements:**

* Has contributed meaningful work to a Strata or has forged an original component adopted into the Guild lineage.
* Demonstrates consistent reliability, quality, and adherence to Guild standards.

**Access & Privileges:**

* Write access to internal repositories aligned with least-privilege principles.
* May build Strata and lead reforges.
* Eligible for royalty slices under the SGL framework.
* May mentor Initiates and Forgers.

**Responsibilities:**

* Uphold Guild ethos, quality, and craft.
* Maintain clear lineage and documentation.

## Artisan

A Builder whose craft displays exceptional quality, vision, and consistency.

**Distinctions:**

* Recognized mastery in one or more technical domains.
* May set standards for patterns, architectures, and utilities.
* May be entrusted with the shaping of future Strata lines or Guild methodologies.

**Privileges:**

* Elevated influence in technical direction and Forge-related decisions.
* May advise in arbitration or lineage evaluation.

## Steward

A Builder elevated into operational, organizational, or governance responsibility.

**Role:**

* Serves the Guild by upholding structure, oversight, arbitration, and orderly function.
* Often (but not always) a W2 employee of Shaleio LLC.

**Responsibilities:**

* Oversight of lineage, reforges, and compliance with the Charter.
* Coordinating contributors, bounties, and internal processes.
* Assisting the Guildmaster in governance.

## Guildmaster

The first Builder and chief custodian of the Charter, ethos, adjudication, and strategic direction of the Guild. The Guildmaster holds final interpretive authority and protects the sovereignty, dignity, and craft of all Builders.
